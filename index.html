About
Peihao Li is a senior at the University of Michigan majoring in Computer Science, Data Science, and Mathematics, with a minor in Music. He is a visiting researcher at Berkeley AI Research (BAIR), where he is grateful for the mentorship of Haoran Geng under the supervision of Prof. Jitendra Malik. He has also been fortunate to collaborate closely with Prof. Pieter Abbeel, Prof. Trevor Darrell, Prof. Junshan Zhang, Prof. Yilun Du, and Prof. Yue Wang. His research interests mainly lie in Robotics and 3D/4D Computer Vision.
